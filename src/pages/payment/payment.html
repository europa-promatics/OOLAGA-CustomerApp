Payment

When do i get billed?

When you book your OOLAGA, a hold is placed on your credit card for the full amount of the OOLAGA. Once your OOLAGA is completed, we charge the card for the total amount. In the event that your cancel your OOLAGA with more than 24 hours notice, your hold is released. If you cancel with less than 24 notice, a 20% cancellation fee is applied.

Can I cancel w/o being charged?

Yes, if your OOLAGA has not been scheduled, you can cancel at any time.
